<p>Microsoft was quick to limit <a href="https://www.engadget.com/microsoft-new-bing-edge-ai-copilot-hands-on-demo-test-chatgpt-openai-213332455.html">Bing's AI chats</a> to prevent <a href="https://www.engadget.com/microsoft-limits-bing-conversations-to-prevent-disturbing-chatbot-responses-154142211.html">disturbing answers</a>, but it's changing course just days later.
